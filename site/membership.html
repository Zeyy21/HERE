FAQ

questions we keep getting

Is Heredita ever paywalled?

No. The core sandbox + alt-history modes stay free forever. Paid tiers add cosmetic + community-hosting tools, not gameplay-defining stuff.

When does pricing get announced?

Same week we open the Store. Watch the Updates page or hit "Notify me" above and we'll email you.

Can I switch tiers later?

Yes — upgrade, downgrade or cancel any time. Anything you've already earned (avatars, titles, coins) stays in your account.

What are Heredita Coins for?

The in-game currency for Store items — flags, hats, brushes, map themes. Coins are also earnable in-game, not pay-only.
